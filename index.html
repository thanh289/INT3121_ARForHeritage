"Exploring Vietnamese Culture Through Augmented Reality"

University of Engineering and Technology, VNU Hanoi

Introduction

Augmented Reality (AR) is transforming the way visitors experience culture, tourism, and museums. Instead of only observing artifacts through static displays, users can now interact with immersive 3D content directly from their devices, creating more engaging and accessible cultural experiences.

This digital museum presents a collection of Vietnamese cultural objects reconstructed as interactive 3D models. Through AR technology, visitors can explore artifacts in real time, examine fine details from every angle, and experience cultural storytelling in immersive virtual environments.

The project also contributes to cultural heritage preservation by digitally archiving traditional artifacts, folk objects, and handcrafted items that may gradually disappear over time. These high-fidelity 3D representations help preserve valuable cultural knowledge while making Vietnamese heritage more accessible to future generations through modern digital experiences.

The Collection

Traditional Musical Instruments: Explore detailed 3D representations of Vietnamese instruments and view their craftsmanship interactively in AR environments.

Water Puppetry Artifacts: Experience iconic puppetry characters as immersive digital exhibits with interactive viewing and storytelling support.

Vietnamese Ceramics: Examine ceramic textures, shapes, and decorative patterns through high-fidelity 3D visualization designed for virtual museum experiences.

Folk Toys & Games: Discover traditional Vietnamese folk objects through engaging AR-based interaction and educational exploration.
