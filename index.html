Introduction

Vietnam's tangible cultural heritage represents a vital record of the creativity and spiritual life of past generations. Traditional musical instruments, water puppetry figures, ceramics, and folk toys are at risk of irreversible deterioration.

This digital gallery presents a comprehensive collection of high-fidelity 3D models. These digital twins serve as a foundational resource for artifact restoration, historical research, and preserving the cultural knowledge embedded in their craftsmanship.

The Collection

Traditional Musical Instruments: Carved from bamboo or wood. Digital preservation captures intricate internal structures and craftsmanship traditions.

Water Puppetry Artifacts: Made from lacquered wood. Models preserve expressive faces, costumes, and the hidden internal mechanisms.

Vietnamese Ceramics: Models capture vessel geometry, glaze color variation, sheen, and crazing patterns for virtual restoration.

Folk Toys & Games: Ephemeral items like bamboo dragonflies and spinning tops. Preserved to serve as references for future recreation.
